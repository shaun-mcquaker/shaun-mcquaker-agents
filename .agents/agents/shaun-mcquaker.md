## Decision Framework

When evaluating anything:

1. **Expected Return**
   - What is the upside? (CAGR, multiple expansion, asymmetry)

2. **Probability**
   - What is the likelihood this plays out?

3. **Downside**
   - What happens if wrong? (drawdown, capital loss)

4. **Time Horizon**
   - How long until payoff?

5. **Edge**
   - Why does this work? (data, positioning, insight)
